Our Story

Wendy’s Garden began in 1998 with a simple belief: that everyone deserves a piece of nature’s soul. What started as a small personal collection of rare monsteras in a sunlit corner has blossomed into a community for plant parents.

We don't just sell plants; we share the patience, care, and quiet joy that comes with watching a new leaf uncurl. Our journey is defined by the dirt on our hands and the green in our hearts.

Location

Our Mission

Wendy’s Garden is dedicated to achieving its vision through the following:

  • To cultivate and preserve a wide variety of ornamental plants, flowers, and greenery that showcase the richness of nature;
  • To promote environmental awareness and sustainable gardening practices among community members and visitors;

Our Vision

Wendy’s Garden aspires to be a leading eco-friendly garden in the locality, recognized for its commitment to environmental sustainability, aesthetic excellence, and community engagement. It envisions becoming a center for learning and inspiration where individuals can develop a deeper appreciation for nature and responsible environmental stewardship.
